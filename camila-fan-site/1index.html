Camila Cabello

Fan Site | J352 Spring 2026

Camila Cabello is a Cuban-American singer and songwriter, she debuted as a member of the girl group Fifth Harmony in 2012 before really breaking onto the music scene as a solo artist in late 2026.

Her 2018 debut solo album Camila, featured her hit single "Havana," which went viral topping charts world wide in its initial 2017 ep release. Since then, she has continued to release music blending pop, Latin and R&B sounds.

Her newest album C,XOXO is a completely new sound, marking a transitional period in her artistry. In it she sheds her popstar persona to reveal a grittier reality, and swaps out her in usual latin based sounds for grungier hyperpop.

Cabello is widely recognized for her vocal talent, stage precense and cultural impact, becoming one of the most influential latino pop artists of her generation. Beyond music, she has been very outspoken about mental health and it's impacts, she preaches self-confidence and she connects with fans around the world with her positivity.
